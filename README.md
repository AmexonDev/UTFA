# European Touch Football Association Regulations 
##### Last Updated: February 28, 2026
--------------------------------------------------
## Section 1 - Gameplay
### Team Requirements
##### 1.0 Each team must have a minimum of 3 players present to begin a match.
##### 1.1 If one team has 3 players and the other has 4, the match will be played as a 4vs3.
##### 1.2 If a team fails to meet the minimum requirement of 3 players, a forfeit win is awarded to the opposing team.
##### 1.3 If both teams fail to meet the minimum requirement in a LEAGUE match, the result is a forfeit draw and a strike is given to both team managers.
##### 1.4 If both teams fail to meet the minimum requirement in a CUP match, the match will be rescheduled and a strike is given to both team managers.
##### 1.5 If a team falls below 3 players during a game, the game will still continue.
##### 1.6 If a player disconnects or leaves during a game, they will receive a verbal warning, followed by a yellow card if repeated.
##### 1.7 A player who disconnects or leaves during a game is allowed to rejoin or be replaced by a registered substitute.
##### 1.8 If the team remains below 3 players by the start of the next game, a forfeit win is awarded to the opposing team and the manager will be striked.
### Forfeit Wins
##### 1.9 A forfeit win is recorded as 2-0.
##### 1.10 A forfeit draw is recorded as 0-0.
##### 1.11 Forfeiting a game will result in a mandatory team vote to sack the manager.
### Ties
##### 1.12 If a cup fixture ends in a tie, the teams will play 2 additional games. This process will repeat until a winner is determined.
##### 1.13 The ‘Sudden Death’ in-game feature is not counted in matches, any goals scored during Sudden Death are voided.
##### 1.14 In CUP matches, Sudden Death is enforced if the aggregate score is tied on the last game.
##### 1.15 If the result remains tied, the process will repeat until a winner is determined.
### Game Requirements
##### 1.16 Games are required to have a referee present.
##### 1.17 If a referee is unavailable, the match may be delayed or rescheduled by mutual agreement of both managers.
##### 1.18 Referees are prohibited from officiating matches involving their own team.
##### 1.19 Each team has the right to host one game.
##### 1.20 Hosting order is determined by a coin flip conducted via a Discord Automated Bot (e.g. carlbot). The winner may choose to host the first or second game.
##### 1.21 After the first game ends, the second game will commence and the non-hosting team will host.
##### 1.22 If the hosting team cannot provide a private server 5 minutes after the coinflip, the referee or the opposing team will provide one. The original hosting team will still host the game. 
### Substitutions
##### 1.23 Substitutions may only be made when the game is paused after a goal is scored, or if the referee pauses the game.
##### 1.24 If a game is paused for any reason, it will only play the remaining time when resumed.
##### 1.25 Players who are substituted out can only re-enter later in the match if their team falls under 4 players in the server.
##### 1.26 Substitutions can only be made by the manager (or assistant if the manager is not present).
### Matchcall
##### 1.27 Team decisions are in a hierarchy, Manager, Assistant, Player. If one is not present, the one in the following order is in charge of representing the team.
##### 1.28 Only active players may be inside of the game link. Substitutes are not permitted inside the server until actively playing.
##### 1.29 Referees and authorized streamers may be present in the private server.
##### 1.30 If a team fields an ineligible player for an entire leg and has prior knowledge of it, the team will get disbanded and the manager will get sanctioned.
##### 1.31 Exploiting in a match results in an immediate unbailable ban.
##### 1.32 If TSW’s Administration fails to assign a Cup-Tied role, responsibility lies with Administration and a rematch may be scheduled.
##### 1.33 Players must screenshare in a designated voice channel with a match official present until the game starts. Or they must screen record their gameplay for the entirety of the match and send the footage after the game. If a screen recording is not sent after the match, the player's stats will be voided and they will be banned from the server until the recording is sent. If the video is not sent for an extended period of time the ban is permanent.
##### 1.34 Match-fixing is strictly prohibited.
##### 1.35 If both Manager and Assistant Manager are absent, managerial duties (substitutions, coin flips) may be temporarily delegated to a volunteer player.
##### 1.36 If a team fails to field the required players, the Manager will receive a Manager Strike.
##### 1.37 Goals deemed impossible or unpredictable may be reviewed by referees and VAR (recordings).
##### 1.38 Players are encouraged to submit video evidence of questionable goals.
##### 1.39Referees may review multiple recordings from different POVs if the goal is found to be hard to decide on and all clips may be submitted to other referees for aid if desired.
##### 1.40 Only goals recognized by the in-game system are eligible for VAR review.
##### 1.41 Match disputes caused by staff or referee error may result in a replay (referee will be deemed responsible
##### 1.42 Managers are responsible for lagging players, and are encouraged to substitute.
##### 1.43 Lag is penalized by a warning, which escalates to a yellow then red card after repeated incidents.
##### 1.44 Time wasting is discouraged, and will result in a yellow card. Repeated incidents will result in a second yellow.
##### 1.45 If a player receives two yellow cards in a game, it is counted as a red card.
##### 1.46 The player who receives a red card must leave the game, and cannot play the next match in that competition.
##### 1.47 Players who receive a red card may be suspended for longer durations depending on the severity of the offense.
## Section 2 - League
### Signing Players
##### **2.01 Leaving the league while on a team will result in a gameban when rejoined.**
##### 2.0 Teams are limited to a certain number of players depending on the division. The capacity for divisions are: Division 1- 9, Division 2- 10, Division 3- 11, Division 4- 12
If a team exceeds the maximum player count, their most recent signing will be voided.
##### 2.1 Managers cannot bypass release clauses or manipulate player values for unfair advantage.
##### 2.2 Any attempt to conceal, alter, or undermine contractual clauses or market values to influence transfers will be considered a breach of league regulations and may result in Manager penalties.
### Fixtures
##### 2.3 Postpones must be requested at least 24 hours before kickoff unless in exceptional circumstances.
##### 2.4 Frequent or unjustified postponements may result in match forfeiture or Manager penalties.
### Cup Tie
##### 2.5 A player who has represented a team in a cup competition cannot play for another team in the same cup during the same season.
##### 2.6 Managers are responsible for ensuring their players are eligible before submitting match rosters.
##### 2.7 Managers who knowingly harbor or recruit alts will face equal or greater penalties.
### Roblox Account
##### 2.8 Players must use their main registered account for all league activities.
##### 2.10 Unauthorized account switching will result in disqualification from active competitions.
##### 2.11 The hierarchy reserves the right to investigate and apply retrospective action if proof emerges later.
### Matches
##### 2.12 All match results are final, with the exception of league administration intervention under special circumstances.
##### 2.13 Match fixing, intentional losses, or manipulation of in-game events are strictly forbidden.
##### 2.14 If a player intentionally sabotages their team (e.g. scoring own goals, being afk, ragequitting) they will be season banned.
##### 2.15 If the private server is shut down mid-game, or an on-field player is kicked, the server owner will be season banned.
##### 2.16 Offenders will receive immediate expulsion from the league and permanent blacklisting.
##### 2.17 Managers and players found complicit will share equal responsibility.
### Results
##### 2.18 All results must be submitted immediately after the match, with clear screenshots or verified confirmation.
##### 2.19 Any falsified result submissions or tampering will result in automatic forfeiture and potential strikes.
##### 2.20 Disputed results must be raised within 24 hours of match completion for official review.
### Rankings
##### 2.22 In order to sign for a team, a player must have an assigned class/tier role.
##### 2.23 The rankings team is in charge of assigning players to classes, a staff member that is not in the rankings team does not have authority in the decisions.
##### 2.24 Players are assigned to classes based on their current ability/skills, which are judged by the previous season’s results, stats, & performances.
##### 2.25 Players are not permanently stuck to a class and may be promoted or demoted based on performances in the current season.
##### 2.26 Players are ranked between 6 tiers: S, A, B, C, D, E.
##### 2.27 Teams are restricted to signing a certain number of players per tier.
##### D1 - A maximum of 3 S tier players. No limit for A tier players. Players under A tier are not allowed in this division.
##### D2 - No limit for B tier players. A maximum of 4 C tier players. Players above B or under C tier are not allowed in this division.
##### D3 - No limit for C tier players. A maximum of 5 D tier players. Players above C or under D tier are not allowed in this division.
##### D4 - No limit for D or E tier players. Players above the D tier are not allowed in this division.
##### 2.28 Players who are unranked are not allowed to sign to a team.
## Section 3 - Fairplay
### Alting & Account Sharing
##### 3.0 Creating alternate accounts (alts) to join multiple teams, influence matches, or manipulate transfers is strictly prohibited.
##### 3.1 Any confirmed case of alting or account sharing will result in an immediate ban(s).
##### 3.2 Players or Managers aware of any alting activity and failing to report it will face penalties for concealment.
### Forging
##### 3.3 Forging evidence regarding contracts, sanctions, or staff investigations is strictly prohibited and will result in a season ban.
## Section 4 - Management
##### 4.0 Managers must ensure the authenticity of all of their signings. Players must be checked for but not limited to: cup tied roles, eligibility within their division
##### 4.1 Managers are expected to uphold professionalism, fairness, and respect toward all players and league officials.
##### 4.2 Managers are responsible for maintaining their team’s discipline and ensuring compliance with all regulations.
##### 4.3 Any Manager found exploiting systems, abusing players, or showing consistent misconduct may be suspended or removed.
### Manager Transfers
##### 4.4 Managers leaving a club mid-season must provide valid reasoning to the hierarchy for approval.
### Responsibilities
##### 4.5 Managers are responsible for team selection, punctuality, and adherence to all scheduling protocols.
##### 4.6 Failure to meet responsibilities may result in managerial strikes or demotion.
### Manager Strikes
##### 4.7 A manager strike will result in a team and managerial investigation by league administration, which may lead to a sacking or team disbandment.
##### 4.8 Two Manager Strikes will result in the Manager being suspended from their role and handed a manager ban.
##### 4.9 A manager ban lasts for an entire season, if it is given mid season it expires on the same matchweek next season.
## Section 5 - Discord
##### Punishments may range from a mute to a ban. Multiple offenses may result in a permanent ban.
### Material
##### 5.0 Advertising is prohibited. This includes direct messages, posting invite links, codes to other leagues, or promoting external services. 
##### 5.1 Posting pornographic, harmful, disturbing, or violent content is strictly prohibited. Punishments may range from a mute to a  ban. Multiple offenses may result in a permanent ban.
##### 5.2 Malicious activities (e.g., doxxing, IP grabbing, malware, spyware) are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate bans.
### Harassment
##### 5.3 Sharing personal information about another user is prohibited. This includes real names, personal photos, addresses etc. Posting this content without the user’s consent or with the intent to cause harm will result in a ban from the server.
##### 5.4 If a user re-shares an already leaked picture of a community member, the affected user may request action by opening a ticket.
##### 5.5 Discrimination based on sex, skin colour, sexuality, disability, age, or stereotypes is prohibited. Islamophobia and antisemitism is not tolerated and will result in sanctions.
##### 5.6 Discriminatory or offensive language, whether in voice or text, is prohibited.
### Soft Infringements
##### 5.7 Excessive pinging or mass mentions are prohibited.
##### 5.8 Spamming GIFs, emojis, text, images, or chat floods is prohibited.
##### 5.9 Using channels incorrectly (e.g., bot commands in general chat) is prohibited.
##### 5.10 Explicit content in images, text, nicknames, or profile pictures is prohibited. No exceptions, even borderline cases.
##### 5.11 Discussions of religion, politics, wars, or other controversial/dramatic topics are prohibited to a certain extent.
##### 5.12 Impersonating another member of the community is prohibited. This does not mean having the same name as someone else.
##### 5.13 Excessive toxicity is not tolerated.
##### 5.14 Posting content that resembles or implies NSFW material will result in a warning. Continued posting after a warning will result in sanctions under Rule 5.1.
### Critical Infringements
##### 5.15 All users must comply with Discord’s Terms of Service and Guidelines:
 https://discord.com/terms
 https://discord.com/guidelines

##### 5.17 Do not request or share personal information (e.g., addresses, phone numbers, emails, age). Protect your privacy and avoid oversharing.
##### 5.18 Content designed to harm others’ health (e.g., earrape, epileptic images, flashing GIFs) is prohibited.
##### 5.19 Discord rules are subject to change at any time. Staff may apply punishments according to their well-documented handbook.
